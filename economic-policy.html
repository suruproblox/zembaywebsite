Economic Policy of the Micronation of Zembay

Article 1: Introduction

The economic policy of Zembay establishes a structured and self-sufficient financial system to promote prosperity, trade, and sustainability within the nation. This policy outlines the currency system, trade regulations, taxation, and economic principles that govern the economy of Zembay.

Article 2: Currency and Financial System

The official currency of Zembay shall be the Zem (ZMB).

The Zembay National Treasury shall oversee the issuance and regulation of the Zem to prevent inflation and ensure financial stability.

Barter systems and foreign currency exchanges may be permitted under the supervision of the Treasury.

Article 3: Trade and Commerce

Citizens and businesses within Zembay are encouraged to engage in both domestic and international trade.

The government may establish trade agreements with other micronations and external entities.

Import and export tariffs shall be determined by the Ministry of Trade to protect local industries and encourage economic growth.

Article 4: Taxation and Revenue

Zembay shall impose a fair taxation system, which may include:

  • Income tax (if applicable) at a reasonable rate.
  • Business taxes for commercial enterprises operating within Zembay.
  • Import/export duties on goods traded outside the nation.

Tax revenues shall be allocated to infrastructure, public services, and national development projects.

Article 5: Industry and Employment

Zembay shall support entrepreneurship, innovation, and self-sufficiency by encouraging local production of goods and services.

Government-backed initiatives may be introduced to provide employment opportunities and economic incentives.

Workersâ€™ rights, including fair wages and safe working conditions, shall be protected under national labor laws.

Article 6: Banking and Financial Institutions

The Zembay National Bank shall oversee monetary policies, regulate banks, and ensure economic stability.

Private and cooperative banking institutions may be permitted to operate under government regulations.

Article 7: Economic Growth and Development

Investment in infrastructure, technology, and renewable energy shall be prioritized to ensure long-term economic sustainability.

The government shall establish economic programs to encourage innovation, research, and digital advancements.

Tourism, culture, and national resources shall be leveraged to boost economic development.

Article 8: Enforcement and Amendments

The Ministry of Finance shall oversee the enforcement of economic policies and ensure compliance.

Amendments to this policy may be proposed by the government and must be approved by the ruling authority of Zembay.

This policy ensures the economic stability and prosperity of Zembay, fostering a self-reliant and thriving nation.
